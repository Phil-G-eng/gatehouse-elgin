The Gatehouse — house rules

No pets allowed on the property at any time.
No smoking, vaping or e-cigarettes anywhere on the property or in the garden.
Quiet hours — please be respectful of neighbours and the surrounding area after 10pm.
Maximum 2 guests — no additional visitors staying overnight.
Check-in: between 15:00 and 20:00  ·  Check-out: before 10:00
Hardwood floors — please protect them. No stiletto heels, heavy boots or dirty outdoor shoes upstairs. Any damage to the flooring will incur charges to cover repairs. The large shelf covering the blocked-off stairwell must be kept completely clear — no heavy weights. Noise levels are monitored.
Reference & ID check: We may ask for a quick reference from a previous host, employer, or personal contact before confirming check-in. We may also request a photo ID upload via our secure verification partner, Stripe Identity. By proceeding you consent to these checks being made.
