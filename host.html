iCal calendar sync

Paste your iCal feed URLs from Airbnb and Booking.com to import their bookings automatically.

How to get your iCal URLs

Airbnb: Go to your listing → Calendar → Availability settings → scroll to "Sync calendars" → copy the Export URL (starts with airbnb.com/calendar/ical/...)

Booking.com: Extranet → Calendar → iCal → Export calendar → copy the URL (starts with admin.booking.com/hotel/hoteladmin/ical...)

Feed URLs

Note: iCal feeds are imported and their booked dates are blocked on your public calendar automatically. Because this dashboard runs locally on your computer, the sync happens when you click "Import now" below. When the site is live on Netlify, syncing will happen automatically every hour.

Your Elgin Lodge iCal export

Use this URL to export your direct bookings back into Airbnb and Booking.com so they block those dates on their platforms too.

https://elginlodge.com/.netlify/functions/export-ical

This will be active once the site is deployed to Netlify.
